THE BAND

That's what "Blind B & The Visionairs" delivers! Some are old. Some are new. All are timeless songs for a great Saturday night out on the town. Songs that stick like honey and make you smile over your drink. And songs that make you want to sing along, move along, and get the evening started. "Blind B & The Visionairs" plays well-known rock songs—from Springsteen to Kravitz, from Stones to Police—with some added flavour of their own. Check out some sound clips to get an impression.

MEMBERS OF THE BAND

SaRon Crenshaw - Bass guitar, lead vocals

Not one to shy away from a challenge, SaRon combines singing with playing the drums. As Call Me Joe’s drummer and lead singer, he manages to lay down a solid beat while unleashing his vocal skills.

Colonel Jetski - Bass guitar

Being the sole band member with just one job, Colonel Jetski takes this very seriously. As blind B and the Visionaires vocally challenged bass player, he puts on his bass face and lays down a solid groove.

Junior Mack - Keys, backing vocals

As the only band member that actually answers to the name, Junior Mack plays the keys while throwing in an occasional backing vocal. Whether on piano, Hammond or Rhodes, Joe squeezes some soul into Call Me Joe.

Bart Kamp Guitar.

Known for his melodical as well as lyrical improvisation skills, Bart is Blind B and the Visionairs and Blind B and the Visionairs can not be without Bart. Rocking his trusty Telecaster, he is the band’s guitarist!
